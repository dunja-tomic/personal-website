Hello World

I'm Dunja! I'm a third year Computer Science student at the University of Waterloo in Waterloo, Canada.

I love all things web development, emerging tech, and self-improvement. I enjoy participating in hackathons and working on meaningful and inventive side projects. I also like board games, podcasts, cooking, and the Oxford comma.

More than anything, I love trying new things and being challenged.

Previous Internships

This summer, I'll be working at Square in Kitchener, where I'll be an Android developer on the Cash App team.

This past fall, I was a software engineering intern at Accenture in Toronto, Canada where my team and I built a proof of concept for verifying the authenticity of high-value designer goods using blockchain technology.

The winter before that I interned as a full stack developer at Hyalto in Ottawa, Canada.

I did my very first co-op term at LoyaltyOne in Toronto, Canada where I worked as a service desk representative.

The GoldenHack

I am the Vice President of Technology for The GoldenHack, a business hackathon based out of Wilfrid Laurier University in Waterloo, ON.

The hackathon had its inaugural event in October 2019 and it was a huge success. We had over 150 participants who all gave overwhelmingly positive feedback on the event.

This year, I'm leading a team of 6 developers & designers in building out a new version of the website for the event, and our very own application system. The whole team is incredibly talented, and I can't wait to see what we accomplish this year.
